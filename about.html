About Me

I’m Prajwal Shrestha, a +2 graduate and an aspiring web developer who’s just beginning his journey into the digital world. Ever since I came across the basics of web development, I’ve been fascinated by how lines of code can bring a vision to life on the screen. I enjoy experimenting with HTML, CSS, and a little bit of JavaScript to create simple yet functional websites. Although my knowledge is still at a beginner level, I believe that every small step forward counts — and I’m eager to keep improving with each project, each challenge, and each lesson I learn along the way. I take pride in being curious, patient, and dedicated, always looking for ways to turn my ideas into something real and meaningful online.

This is just the beginning of my journey, but I’m determined to keep learning, take on new challenges, and sharpen my skills day by day. I hope to grow into a developer who creates websites that are not just functional, but also creative and inspiring.
